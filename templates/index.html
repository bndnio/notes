Notes

Capture notes to your Notion database — by email or from your AI assistant.

How it works

  • Send an email to your personal address u_your-username@bndn.io — subject becomes title, body becomes content.
  • Use the MCP server with Claude to save notes directly from your conversations.
  • Everything lands in your own Notion database.

Getting started

  • Create a Notion integration and share your database with it.
  • Register below with your email, a username, your Notion Database ID, and your integration token.
  • You'll receive an MCP token to configure the server, and your email address will be authorised to send notes.
